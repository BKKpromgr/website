The Bangkok suvarnabhumi Airport

Suvarnabhumi Airport is the largest airport in Thailand and serves around 12 million people per year. The airport is home to airlines like Thai Airways and Bangkok Airways with Thai being the largest one. The airport is devided in to two buildings, the main terminal building (MTB) and the satelite terminal (SAT 1) with pland for an extention in the future. The MBT is divided in to seven different concuorses, ant the SAT 1 is a long line of terminal and is connected to the MBT by an underground tram.

The airport was finished in 2006 and is said to be haunted as it was buildt on a former graveyard. The aiport has two runways that supports large aircraft like the Airbus 380
